-->Introduction to large language models
-->What is a Large Language Model (LLM)?
An LLM is an AI model trained on huge amounts of text to predict the next word and generate human-like responses.
-->Training Data,Tokens-Text is broken into small pieces called tokens, which the model processes one by one.
-->Next Token Prediction – The model learns by predicting the next token in a sentence.
-->Neural networks are used to learn relationships between words and sentences.
-->Pretraining teaches the model general knowledge from large amounts of text.
-->Prompting guides the model by providing instructions or examples.
-->Large Language Models can perform tasks such as writing, summarization, and translation.
-->Model evaluation measures the quality and accuracy of generated outputs.
-->Large Language Models are powerful tools but still require human supervision and verification.
